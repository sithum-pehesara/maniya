Who is ManiYa?

Manitha Abeysiriwardana, widely known as ManiYa, is one of Sri Lanka's most prominent digital content creators. Known for his high-energy, sarcastic, and wildly entertaining videos, he has captivated a massive audience across multiple platforms.

From parody videos and meme reviews to thrilling gaming streams featuring "The B Gang", ManiYa's unique style has redefined the Sri Lankan YouTube landscape. In early 2020, he was recognized as the Most Popular Upcoming YouTube Channel at the Raigam Social Media Awards.
